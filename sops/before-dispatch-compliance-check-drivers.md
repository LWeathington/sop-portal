# Before Dispatch Compliance Check (Drivers)

**Driver Safety & Hours of Service (HOS) Compliance Verification**

- **Company:** Spot On TMS
- **Department:** Safety & Compliance
- **Applies To:** All Spot On TMS drivers and all personnel responsible for driver safety, compliance, and dispatch authorization.

## Purpose

The purpose of this Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) is to ensure that all Spot On TMS drivers are safe, compliant, and legally eligible to operate in accordance with FMCSA Hours of Service (HOS) regulations.

This SOP establishes a standardized process for reviewing, correcting, documenting, and approving driver compliance using the Samsara ELD system prior to dispatch or continued operation.

## Scope

This SOP applies to:
- All Spot On TMS drivers operating company-owned or leased equipment
- All Safety, Compliance, and Operations personnel responsible for driver clearance

No driver may be authorized to operate if they are found to be non-compliant with this procedure.

## Responsibility

**Safety & Compliance Personnel** are responsible for:
- Conducting daily HOS and compliance reviews
- Assigning unassigned logs accurately and promptly
- Communicating required corrections to drivers immediately
- Documenting all actions taken
- Preventing non-compliant drivers from operating

**Drivers** are responsible for:
- Accepting and certifying all assigned logs without delay
- Maintaining accurate and compliant ELD records
- Cooperating with safety coaching and corrective actions

## Procedure

### Step 1: Access Samsara System

- Log in to the Samsara ELD platform using authorized Spot On TMS credentials.
- Verify access to Compliance and Reporting dashboards.

### Step 2: Navigate to Compliance Dashboard

- Open the Compliance Dashboard in Samsara.
- Review the high-level compliance overview, including:
  - Hours of Service (HOS) Violations
  - Unassigned Driving
  - Unassigned Segments

### Step 3: Verify Compliance Status

- Confirm that HOS Violations, Unassigned Driving, and Unassigned Segments all display 100% compliance.
- If any indicator is below 100%, select "View Details" and proceed with corrective action.

### Step 4: Review and Correct Unassigned Hours of Service

- Open the Unassigned Hours of Service report.
- Set the report timeframe to one (1) week.
- Assign all unassigned HOS to the correct Spot On TMS driver.
- Contact the driver immediately and instruct them to accept and certify the logs.

### Step 5: Review and Correct Unassigned Driving

- Navigate to Unassigned Driving and select View Details.
- Assign all unassigned driving time to the correct Spot On TMS driver.
- Contact the driver immediately and require acceptance and certification.

### Step 6: Final Review, Documentation, and Driver Authorization

- Document all actions taken.
- Verify the driver's current HOS status.
- If compliant, clear the driver to operate.
- If violations or safety issues exist, notate the unit, remove the driver from service if required, and conduct immediate coaching.

## Enforcement

Failure to comply with this SOP may result in FMCSA violations, increased CSA risk, and corrective or disciplinary action.

## Recordkeeping

All documentation related to compliance checks, log corrections, and driver coaching must be retained per Spot On TMS and FMCSA record retention requirements.
