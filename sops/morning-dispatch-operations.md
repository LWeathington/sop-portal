# Morning Dispatch Operations

## Purpose

To execute morning dispatch activities including planning, driver readiness, load coverage, and proactive communication.

## Scope

Applies to assigned personnel performing morning dispatch functions.

## Prerequisites

Completion of Upon Arrival - First LogIns SOP

## Procedure

## DITAT (TMS) Updates

(refer to Daily Time Updates)

### A. Complete Trips from Previous Day

- Close out all completed trips
- Confirm:
  - Delivery status
  - POD received (if applicable)
  - Accurate times (PU/DEL)
  - Correct equipment and driver assigned
- Verify trip financial data is accurate before completion
**Timing Expectation:** Completed by 0800.

### B. Start Trips for Current Day that have NOT been auto-dispatched

- Activate all scheduled trips
- Confirm:
  - Correct driver assignment
  - Equipment/trailer accuracy
  - Pickup appointments verified
- Ensure trip status reflects actual dispatch plan
**Timing Expectation:** Completed by 0800.

### C. Update Trip Changes, Delays, or Exceptions

Immediately update DITAT for:
- Appointment changes
- Late arrivals
- Breakdowns
- Re-power situations
- Equipment swaps
- Cancellations
- Fines/Fees

### Update Trailer Assignments in Ditat

- Reflect correct driver and load pairing
- Remove outdated assignments

### Verify Accuracy - If trailer location or assignment is unclear:

- Confirm with dispatch or driver
- Do not assume or estimate location
**All system updates must reflect real-time operational change**
**Communicate with Customers**
**Customer/Driver Info Update**

### Issue Communication

- Notify brokers/customers of all known issues
- Communicate clearly:
  - What happened
  - Why it happened
  - How it will be resolved
  - Document communications

### Planning & Load Coverage for Current Day

- Update dispatch plan
- Identify "must-cover" needs:
  - drivers needing backhauls
  - time-sensitive freight
  - local priorities

- Search and post loads on approved platforms (refer to Needs List Management )

### Driver Readiness

- Review driver schedules and HOS
  - Open Samsara
  - Compliance
  - Check Compliance Dashboard for any Violations
- Contact drivers starting within 30 minutes Company / Dispatch Info
- Identify and address delays or issues
Confirm PU# is in the load.

## Escalation Standards

- Unresponsive driver: escalate after 5 minutes
- Service failure risk: escalate immediately
- If unsure, contact DITAT support: 314‑244‑3801

## Output

- Drivers are confirmed and operating
- Loads are covered or actively being worked
