# Afternoon Dispatch Operations

## Purpose

To finalize dispatch plans, confirm next-day operations, and close out dispatch activities accurately.

## Scope

Applies to assigned personnel responsible for afternoon and end-of-day dispatch operations.

## Procedure

### Driver Check-Ins

- Contact drivers for status updates and concerns

### Plan Finalization

- Review next-day coverage
- Resolve open gaps
- Confirm load commitments

### Customer Communication

- Provide next-day ETAs
- Confirm delivery expectations
- Document confirmations

### Final Load Coverage

- Perform final load searches as needed

### Dispatch Close-Out

- Confirm all systems are current
- Send final updates to drivers and brokers
- Verify next-day plan is clearly communicated

## Close-Out Standards

- No undocumented issues
- All systems accurate
- Next-day plan clearly defined

## Output

A finalized and communicated dispatch plan for the next business day.
