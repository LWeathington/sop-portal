# Entering a Cargill Load

## Start a New Shipment

1.  Log in to **Ditat TMS**.
2.  Click the **Shipments** tab.
3.  Select **New Shipment** to create a new load.
- Enter Customer Information

4.  In the **Customer** field, type and select **Cargill**.
5.  Enter the **Cargill load or confirmation number** (from the load tender).
6.  Enter any required **reference numbers** provided by Cargill.
- Enter Pickup (Origin) Details

7.  Enter the **pickup location** listed on the Cargill load confirmation. Most common pick up point is CFNKC.
8.  Select the correct pickup address from the list.
9.  Set the **pickup appointment type**:
    - Scheduled or FCFS (as shown on the tender).
10. Enter the **pickup date and time**.
- Enter Delivery (Destination) Details

11. Enter the **delivery location** listed on the load.
12. Select the correct delivery address.
13. Set the **delivery appointment type**.
14. Enter the **delivery date and time**.

## Commodity & Load Details

15. Enter the **commodity description** (as shown on the Cargill confirmation).
16. Enter the **weight** of the load.
17. Verify any **special instructions** (food-grade, seals, temperature, etc.).

## Rate & Charges

18. Go to the **Charges** section.
19. Add a **Freight charge**.
20. Enter the **rate agreed with Cargill**.
21. Confirm the total charge amount is correct.

## Document Management

22. Open **Document Management**.
23. Attach the **Cargill rate confirmation / load tender**.
24. Confirm the document is attached to the shipment.

## Final Review

25. Review:
- Customer = Cargill
- Pickup & delivery locations
- Dates & appointment times
- Weight & commodity
- Rate & charges
26. Confirm all required fields are complete.

## Save the Load

27. Click **Save** or **Save & Close** to finish entering the Cargill load.
