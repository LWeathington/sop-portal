# Customer / Driver Info Update

## Purpose

To ensure customers receive accurate and timely driver information for scheduled loads.

## Scope

Applies to assigned personnel responsible for communicating driver details to customers once a load is confirmed.

## Systems Used

- TMS (e.g., DITAT)
- Gmail / Email system
- (Optional) Google Sheets or internal tracking tools
- Ringcentral

## Procedure

### Step 1: Open the Load in the TMS

1.  Log into the TMS system
2.  Locate the correct load using:
    - Load number
    - Driver name
    - Date
3.  Open the load details screen

### Step 2: Verify Driver Information

Confirm the following driver details are correct:
- Driver name
- Truck number
- Trailer number (if applicable)
- Phone number
- Pickup date and time
- Delivery date and time
If information is missing or incorrect:
- Update it in the TMS before proceeding
- Confirm changes are saved

### Step 3: Copy Driver Information

1.  Highlight the required driver details from the load
2.  Copy the information for use in customer communication

### Step 4: Open Customer Email

1.  Open Gmail (or assigned email system)
2.  Locate:
    - Existing email thread for the load **OR**
    - Create a new email if no thread exists
3.  Verify the correct customer contact email address

### Step 5: Send Driver Information to Customer

In the email body, include:
- Load or reference number
- Driver name
- Truck number
- Trailer number (if applicable)
- Driver phone number
- Pickup and delivery information
Use clear, professional formatting to ensure the information is easy to read.

### Step 6: Review Before Sending

Before sending:
- Confirm driver information matches the TMS
- Confirm correct customer recipient
- Check for typos or missing information

### Step 7: Send Email

- Send the email to the customer
- Confirm the email sends successfully

### Step 8: Document Communication

1.  Return to the TMS
2.  Note that driver information was sent to the customer
3.  Include:
    - Date and time sent
    - Method (email)
    - Any special notes if applicable

## Error Handling

- If customer email bounces or fails:
  - Verify correct email address
  - Resend once corrected
- If driver information changes after sending:
  - Send an updated email immediately
  - Document the update in the TMS
- If driver fails or is late:
  - Send an updated email immediately

## Output

- Customer has received accurate driver information
- Communication is documented
- Load is cleared for execution
