# Employee Onboarding (General)

This document outlines the standard procedure for welcoming and onboarding new employees into the organization. Following this protocol ensures that all new hires receive a consistent, high-quality experience.

## Pre-Arrival Preparation

Before the employee's first day, the HR and IT departments must coordinate the following:
- **Create User Accounts**: Provision email, Slack, and Active Directory accounts.
- **Hardware Setup**: Configure the laptop with the standard organizational image.
- **Welcome Package**: Place the new hire welcome package (swag, handbook) on their designated desk.
> *Tip: Ensure the laptop is fully charged and all system updates are pre-installed before their first day.*

## Day 1: Welcome and Orientation

The first day should be focused on culture, team building, and basic administrative tasks.
1. **9:00 AM** - Office tour and introduction to the immediate team.
2. **10:00 AM** - Meeting with HR to complete I-9, W-4, and benefits enrollment via the portal.
3. **12:00 PM** - Team lunch (Company sponsored).
4. **2:00 PM** - IT Orientation (Security training, password policies).
