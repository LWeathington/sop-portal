# Building a Load in Ditat TMS

1.  **Open Ditat TMS**
    - Navigate to your Ditat TMS login page (e.g., https://ng01.ditat.net/).
2.  **Go to Shipments**
    - Click the **Shipments** tab.
3.  **Create a New Shipment**
    - Click **New Shipment**.
4.  **Prepare Source Data**
    - Locate the rate confirmation or load info from your email or document.
    - Copy the **load number or confirmation number** for reference.
5.  **Enter Basic Load Details**
    - In the new shipment screen, enter the **Customer/Broker name** and select it.
    - Enter the **Confirmation number** (from the rate con).
    - Enter the **Shipper pickup reference number**.
    - Enter the **Receiver delivery reference number**.
6.  **Enter Origin and Destination**
    - Enter the **Pick-up location** and select the correct match.
    - Enter the **Delivery location** and select the correct match.
7.  **Appointment Settings**
    - Choose the appropriate **appointment type** (e.g., FCFS, Scheduled).
    - Set the **pickup date and time**.
    - Set the **delivery date and time**.
8.  **Commodity and Weight**
    - Enter the **commodity/product type** (e.g., General Freight).
    - Enter the **weight** from the rate confirmation.
9.  **Add Charges**
    - Click **Add Charge**.
    - Select the appropriate **charge type** for customer billing.
    - Enter the **agreed rate** for this load.
10. **Document Management**
    - Open **Document Management**.
    - Attach the **signed confirmation or rate con** PDF.
    - Exit Document Management.
11. **Save Load**
    - **Save and close** the shipment.
