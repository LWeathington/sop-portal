# Driver Orientation — Step by Step

**Department:** HR & Onboarding
**Owner:** Austin Boerckel (operations) + Lindsay Weathington (paperwork)
**Effective:** April 2026

---

## Overview

This SOP covers the complete process for bringing a new driver into Spot On Logistics — from interview through their first dispatch. Austin owns the operational side; Lindsay owns the compliance and paperwork side. Both must complete their steps before a driver is dispatched.

---

## Phase 1: Screening & Hiring

**Owner: Austin Boerckel**

### Step 1: Application Review (Tenstreet)
1. Austin reviews applications in Tenstreet
2. Minimum requirements:
   - **2+ years of verifiable CDL driving experience**
   - Clean MVR (no DUI, no reckless — review case by case)
   - Valid CDL-A
   - No more than 2 preventable accidents in 3 years
3. Austin contacts qualified candidates to schedule an interview

### Step 2: Phone/Video Screen
Austin conducts a 15-minute screen covering:
- Driving experience and equipment familiarity
- Availability and home time expectations
- Understanding of HOS rules
- Any red flags (attitude, communication issues)

### Step 3: Road Test
1. Austin schedules an in-person road test
2. Driver brings valid CDL and medical card
3. Austin evaluates: pre-trip inspection knowledge, shifting, backing, safe driving
4. Austin documents road test results

### Step 4: Drug Screen
1. Austin sends driver to **Express Labs** for pre-employment drug screen
2. Drug screen must clear before any offer is made
3. Austin tracks result — do not proceed until a negative result is confirmed

### Step 5: Blake Approval
Austin presents the candidate to Blake with:
- Driving history summary
- Road test notes
- Drug screen clearance confirmation
- Recommended pay rate

**Blake gives final approval before an offer is extended.**

---

## Phase 2: Compliance Paperwork

**Owner: Lindsay Weathington**

Once Blake approves the hire, Lindsay handles all paperwork **before the driver's first day**.

### Step 1: Create Compliance File
Lindsay creates a physical and digital folder for the new driver containing:
- Driver application (from Tenstreet)
- MVR (Motor Vehicle Record)
- PSP (Pre-employment Screening Program) report
- Drug screen results
- Medical examiner's certificate (DOT physical card)
- Copy of CDL (front and back)
- Copy of Social Security card and ID

### Step 2: Collect Signed Policy Forms
On or before Day 1, Lindsay collects signatures on:
- Driver Policy Agreement
- Drug & Alcohol Policy Acknowledgement
- Electronic Logging Device (ELD) Agreement
- Direct deposit/payroll form
- Emergency contact form

**No driver is dispatched until these forms are signed and filed.**

### Step 3: Set Up in Payroll
1. Lindsay adds driver to payroll system (Paychex or QB)
2. Confirms pay rate matches what Blake approved
3. Sets up direct deposit if applicable

---

## Phase 3: System Setup

**Owner: Austin Boerckel**

### Step 1: Add Driver to Ditat
Follow the **Adding a New Driver to Ditat SOP**.
Confirm: Name, CDL number, home terminal, equipment type.

### Step 2: Add Driver to Samsara
Follow the **Adding a New Driver to Samsara SOP**.
Confirm: Driver profile, HOS settings, assigned vehicle (if applicable).

### Step 3: Equipment Assignment
1. Austin assigns a truck (if company vehicle) or confirms driver's truck setup
2. Austin confirms ELD is functioning in the assigned truck
3. Austin walks driver through Samsara ELD basics if needed

---

## Phase 4: Day 1 Orientation

**Owner: Austin Boerckel**

### Morning of Day 1
1. Austin greets the driver and introduces them to the team
2. Austin reviews:
   - Daily dispatch routine and expectations
   - Check-in / check-call process
   - How to report issues (breakdowns, accidents, delays)
   - Communication expectations (text response time, check-in frequency)
3. Austin walks driver through the pre-trip inspection process (company standards)

### Key Topics Covered
| Topic | Who Explains |
|-------|-------------|
| Dispatch process and load assignment | Austin |
| Samsara ELD and GPS use | Austin |
| Check-call expectations | Austin |
| Breakdown and emergency procedures | Austin |
| Payroll schedule and fuel card use | Lindsay |
| Drug and alcohol policy review | Lindsay |
| Who to call for what | Austin |

### Step 5: First Load Dispatch
1. Austin assigns driver a starter load (ideally shorter, straightforward run)
2. Austin monitors closely for the first 2–3 loads
3. Austin follows up after first delivery
4. Austin flags any concerns to Blake immediately

---

## Checklist — Do Not Dispatch Without These

- [ ] Road test completed and documented
- [ ] Drug screen — negative result confirmed
- [ ] Blake approval received
- [ ] Compliance file created (Lindsay)
- [ ] All policy forms signed (Lindsay)
- [ ] Added to Ditat (Austin)
- [ ] Added to Samsara (Austin)
- [ ] Equipment assigned and ELD verified
- [ ] Day 1 orientation completed
