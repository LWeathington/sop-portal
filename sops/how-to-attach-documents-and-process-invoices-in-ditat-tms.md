# How To Attach Docs & Process Invoices in Ditat

### Attach Scanned Documents (BOL/POD) to Loads

1.  Open **Ditat TMS** and log in.
2.  Click the **Bell (notifications)** icon.
3.  Open any **message with a paperclip icon** (indicates an attachment).
4.  Click the **scanned document** (e.g., BOL/POD).
5.  Click **Attach** to begin attaching it to the correct load.
6.  **Select the correct load** --- verify pickup numbers, load number, etc.
7.  Choose the correct **paperwork category** (e.g., POD).
8.  Click **Finish**.
9.  Click **OK** and repeat until all bell notifications are cleared.

### Create a New Invoice Batch

10. Go to the **Rate & Pay** menu.
11. Click **Process invoices/memos**.
12. Click **NewBatch** to make a new invoice batch.
13. Click **Save**.
14. Click add shipment
15. Select proof of delivery box and check Yes
16. Hit search
17. Checkmark all shipments you want to add, then hit okay
18. After confirming all are bill VIA Email, hit post
19. To check they all went correctly, select print summary. Once complete, select okay
