# Daily Checklist

## Morning Dispatch Checklist

## 6:00--6:15 \| Office & System Startup

- Unlock doors and turn on lights
- Start computer and log into all systems
- Review Samsara:
  - HOS status
  - Previous week violations
  - Camera alerts/issues

## 6:15--6:25 \| Driver Readiness Check

- Check all driver statuses and HOS
- Confirm drivers are on time
- Call:
  - Any driver scheduled to move within the next 30 minutes
  - Any driver showing delays or issues

## 6:25--6:35 \| Customer & Broker Updates

- Notify customers/brokers of **all** issues
- Clearly communicate:
  - What happened
  - Why it happened
  - How it will be fixed
- Document all communication in dispatch systems

## 6:35--6:45 \| System Updates (DITAT)

- Complete previous day trips
- Start trips for the current day
- Update any changes or issues
- If unsure, contact DITAT support: 314‑244‑3801

## 6:45--7:00 \| Planning & Needs Setup

- Finalize which drivers are working the next day
- Begin next‑day planning
- Identify coverage gaps and load needs
- Send needs list for:
  - Current day
  - Next 5--7 business days

## 7:00--7:30 \| Dispatch Plan & Load Coverage

- Update dispatch plan
- Set up next‑day needs
- Search for loads
- Post trucks on DAT and Manifold
- Post for both partial and full truckload

## 7:30--8:00 \| Stand‑To‑Start Call

- Participate in Stand‑To‑Start call
- Address overnight issues and priorities

## 8:00--9:00 \| Load Optimization

- Update dispatch plan
- Continue load searching across all platforms
- Post trucks as needed (DAT, Manifold)

## 9:00--9:30 \| Driver Status & Needs Update

- Check driver status and on‑time performance
- Identify delays or issues
- Report issues to current and next‑load brokers
- Send updated needs list

## 9:30--11:15 \| Ongoing Load Search & Updates

- Continuous load searching and posting
- Update DITAT as needed
- Send needs list

## 11:15--13:00 \| Midday Load Coverage

- Continue load searching on all platforms
- Post trucks for uncovered needs

## 13:00--13:30 \| Driver Check‑Ins

- Check in with drivers
- Address concerns, needs, or updates

## 13:30--13:45 \| Equipment Tracking

- Update trailer sheet

## 13:45--14:15 \| Final Planning

- Analyze and finalize next‑day plan

## 14:15--14:30 \| Customer ETA Calls

- Call Cargill customers
- Provide ETAs for next day deliveries

## 14:30--15:30 \| Final Load Coverage

- Continue load searching
- Ensure all gaps are covered

## 15:30--16:30 \| Dispatch Close‑Out

- Update dispatch board
- Send final needs list
- Dispatch confirmed loads
- Send driver and broker updates
- Confirm everyone understands the plan
- Ensure all systems are fully updated
**Standards Reminder**
- Be proactive, clear, and factual
- Escalate unresponsive drivers after 15 minutes
- Notify management immediately if service failure is at risk
