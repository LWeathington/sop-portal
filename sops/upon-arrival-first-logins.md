# Upon Arrival: First Log-Ins

## Purpose

To ensure all required systems are accessed, operational, and ready before dispatch activities begin.

## Scope

Applies to any assigned personnel responsible for preparing dispatch systems at the start of the business day.

## Required Systems

- Gmail
- Google Sheets- Company/Dispatch Information Sheet- Planning Board Sheet
- DAT One
- Google Maps
- Samsara
- Manifold
- DITAT

## Procedure

1.  **Open Google Chrome**
2.  **Open Gmail (Dispatch@Spotontms.com)**
3.  **Open Google Sheets**
    - Open Google Apps(Nine Dots)
    - Select the appropriate Google Sheets needed: **Company/Dispatch Information Sheet- Planning Board Sheet**
4.  **DAT One**
    - Open a new browser tab
    - Navigate to [one.dat.com/search-loads](http://one.dat.com/search-loads)
    - Log in and confirm access
      i.  Refer to Company / Dispatch Info for pw reference
5.  **Google Maps**
    - Open a new tab
    - Navigate to [google.com/maps](http://google.com/maps)
6.  **Samsara**
    - Open a new tab
    - Log into the Samsara dashboard
    - Verify fleet visibility and data is loading
7.  **Manifold**
    - Open a new tab
    - Navigate to https://loadboard.manifoldfreight.com/
    - Log in successfully
8.  **DITAT**
    - Open a new tab
    - Navigate to ng01.ditat.net/login
    - Log in and confirm dashboard access
      i.  Account ID - Spotontms
      ii. Username -
      iii. Password -

## Output

Systems are fully operational and ready for dispatch activities.
